SADBOYSARCHIVE

Sad Boys, also known as Sad Boys Entertainment (SBE) are a rap collective from Sweden. The group consists of rapper Yung Lean and producers Yung Sherman and Gud.

In 2012, Jonatan Leandoer Håstad met Carl-Mikael Berlander and Axel Tufvesson in a park in Stockholm. They bonded over their shared music taste and decided to form a rap collective, with Håstad taking the name Yung Lean, Berlander going by Yung Gud, and Tufvesson as Yung Sherman.

In 2013 they quickly rose to prominence after Yung Lean's track Ginseng Strip 2002 went viral online.

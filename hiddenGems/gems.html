SADBOYSARCHIVE

The hidden gems page is a compilation of less known songs from the SBE group that are worth mentioning. Anybody can find the mainstream songs. The songs below are the true hidden gems that are often being gatekeeped within the GTBSBE community.

‍

Yung Lean - Outta my head [2020]

The most known song by Yung Lean on this list, but still very underrated compared to his most famous songs. This song shines through with the very well done music video, great vocals, lyrics and music video.

‍

Yung Lean - Orroz [2014]

An unreleased song from Yung Lean produced by Yung Gud alias Gud. It was rumored that this songs should have ended up on the Unknown Death album, but for unknown reasons was never published with it.

‍

Yung Sherman - Drown Me [2012/2013]

A solo track by the Yung Sherman made sometime in 2012/2013. Very futuristic for that time.

‍

Yung Lean - Vendetta [2017]

One of the more known songs on this list, but still very underrated compared to his other mainstream songs.

‍

Dod Mark (Yung Lean) - Vit Sprit [2014]

Dod Mark is a project of Yung Lean where he sings in his own language which is Swedish. This song is done in the metal genre which shows just how versatile Jonatan is.

‍

Jonatan Leandoer96 (Yung Lean) - Highway [2021]

Jonatan Leandoer96 is another project of Yung Lean where his songs mainly compose of softer beats and vocals. This is one of his personas where he is not limiting his creativity to satisfy the mainstream consumers.
